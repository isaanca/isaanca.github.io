Nueva School Math Circle

Our first event was at the October 2018 math circle at the Nueva School, an event open to the community. We hosted a programming workshop where students in third through eighth grade spent a fun afternoon learning how the internet worked through a note-passing simulation with index cards, and solved programming puzzles.

We came back to the Nueva Math Circle for their October 2019 event, where we ran an adaptation of our cryptology escape room puzzle for middle schoolers. After a lot of testing from the club, this event was a success! Students cracked several types of codes to follow in the footsteps of cryptanalyst Elizebeth Smith Friedman.
